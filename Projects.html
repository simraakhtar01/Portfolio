Project 1: Automated Auto Shop Management Software

Details
The project aims to revolutionize the auto shop industry by introducing an automated management software designed to streamline operations and enhance customer experience. Through extensive stakeholder interviews, our team identified common pain points faced by auto shops, leading to the development of a comprehensive solution that addresses these issues.
Objectives:
Our primary goal is to automate and simplify the daily tasks of auto shops, offering features such as oil product selection, service record keeping, low-quantity alarms, sales tracking, discount management, and invoice generation. By doing so, we aim to assist customers in making informed decisions and ensure a seamless service experience.
Conclusion:
In summary, our Workshop Management Project offers a comprehensive software solution to address key challenges in auto repair shops. By streamlining operations, enhancing customer engagement, and optimizing workflow, we aim to revolutionize the way workshops operate. With intuitive features and ongoing improvements, our project promises to drive efficiency and maximize customer satisfaction in the auto repair industry.
